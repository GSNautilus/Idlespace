Slots beyond population are locked. Slots marked (auto) are filled by the sim from drawn cards; manually-placed cards are sticky.

Research

Auto-research starts a new cycle each time none is active. On completion, rolls 3 candidates per the real-game distribution (66% tech per slot). Sim always picks the highest-scoring non-tech; skips cycle if 0 non-tech.

Pick Priorities

Weights drive the sim's research and draw-pick decisions, and slot replacement. All 1.0 = balanced. Crank credits = expansion focus, etc.
